Angela's Cinammon Roll Recipie

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour
    • 🤣😋😍

For the Filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1½ tablespoons ground cinammon
  • ¼ butter

Ingredients

  1. Mix milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs. Melt the Buter and add to the mixture.
  2. Add in flour and mix until combind into a dough. Kneed for 10 minutes.
  3. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  4. After the dough has doubled in size roll it out into a rectangle
  5. Melt the butter filling and mix in eith the sugar and cinammon.
  6. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  7. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll into a swiss roll.
  8. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake rolls for 20-25 minutes until lightly brown.
